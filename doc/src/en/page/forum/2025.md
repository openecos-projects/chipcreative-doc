# ChipCreative Forum 2025
## Introduction
In 2025, open-source chip teams from China achieved remarkable progress in areas such as commercial chip deployment, full agile design flows, and talent cultivation. Notably, the **55nm open-source PDK jointly released by ICsprout Integrated Circuit Co., Ltd. (hereinafter referred to as "ICsprout") and the ECOS Team** became the most important piece completing the open-source chip ecosystem (manufacturing direction) in China! Therefore, to share this exciting news with the broader community and systematically present the phased achievements of the ECOS Team and its partners over the past year, **the second ChipCreative Forum will be held on January 25, 2026, at the Shuibo Campus of Zhejiang University Hangzhou Global Scientific and Technological Innovation Center (due to force majeure, the forum was postponed to early 2026).** The forum is organized by the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Beijing Institute of Open Source Chip, and hosted by Zhejiang University Hangzhou Global Scientific and Technological Innovation Center, ICsprout Integrated Circuit Co., Ltd. Teachers, students, and engineers interested in open-source chips are welcome to actively register and participate.

The theme of this forum is **"Full Process of Open Source Chip Design"**, with discussions focusing on open-source IP/SoC (Xiangshan processor, Baiyang DDR controller), open-source EDA (OpenROAD, ECC), open-source PDK (ICsprout 55nm), design platforms (ECOS Studio), and talent cultivation ("One Student One Chip"). In addition, the forum features three major highlights: **First, ICsprout will officially introduce its 55nm open-source PDK and supporting tapeout services. Second, the world's first fully open-source 55nm chip will be unveiled.** During the coffee break, the ECOS Team will set up a booth to publicly display the first fully open-source chip developed based on ICsprout's 55nm process, along with companion demo boards and embedded applications (Mambo voice robot, retro game console). **Third, all attendees will have the valuable opportunity to tour the ICsprout 12-inch pilot line for free**, experiencing the charm of chip manufacturing up close.
