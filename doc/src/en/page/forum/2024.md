# ChipCreative Forum 2024
## Introduction
With the rapid development of open-source chip technology, the field of chip design is witnessing unprecedented innovation opportunities. In this domain, China has achieved remarkable breakthroughs in areas such as open-source instruction sets and open-source EDA. **On December 21, the inaugural ChipCreative Forum will be held at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou).** The forum is organized by the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Beijing Institute of Open Source Chip, and hosted by IEEE CEDA (GZ), the Microelectronics Thrust of HKUST(GZ), and the Guangzhou Key Laboratory of EDA. Renowned experts and scholars from industry and academia will gather to discuss the latest research progress, challenges, opportunities, and future trends of open-source concepts in chip design.

ChipCreative is jointly formed by several well-known organizations in the chip design field. With open-source as its core philosophy, it is committed to building a complete open-source chip design solution and its technical ecosystem. By reforming chip design methodologies through open-source principles, it strives to achieve the ultimate vision of **"designing open-source chips using open-source EDA tools and open-source IP."** This forum is an excellent opportunity to gain in-depth understanding of cutting-edge technologies and the latest developments in open-source chips and open-source EDA tools. The forum is free and open to the public. We sincerely invite all experts, teachers, and students interested in open-source chip design to participate. We look forward to exploring the infinite possibilities of open-source chips with you at the forum on December 21!

### Transportation
- **Destination:** The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou)
- **Address:** No. 1 Duxue Road, Nansha District, Guangzhou, Guangdong Province
- **Options:** There are generally two ways to arrive in Guangzhou from other provinces: by plane or by high-speed rail (excluding long-distance self-driving). Below are the best transportation options to reach the HKUST(GZ) campus (if you reside in Guangzhou city proper, please use a navigation app to choose a suitable route):
    - **By Air**
        - **Option 1 (Ride-hailing/Taxi):** Board a ride-hailing vehicle or taxi at the designated area of Baiyun Airport and set the destination to **"HKUST(GZ)"**. Due to the distance, the trip is expected to take about 80-100 minutes and the cost is relatively high (for reference only, actual conditions may vary).
        - **Option 2 (Subway + Shuttle):** Take Metro Line 3 (North Extension) from **"Airport South Station"** or **"Airport North Station"** to **"Tiyu Xilu Station"**, transfer to Line 3 to **"Kecun Station"**, then transfer to Line 8 to **"Wanshengwei Station"**, and finally transfer to Line 4 to **"Dongchong Station"**. After exiting the station, walk about 10 minutes to reach the campus.
    - **By High-Speed Rail**
        - **Option 1 (Departing from Qingsheng Station):** If your train stops at **"Qingsheng Station"**, a taxi ride takes only about 10 minutes, this is the most convenient option.
        - **Option 2 (Departing from Guangzhou South Station):** Take a ride-hailing vehicle or taxi to the university, about 40-50 minutes. For public transport, take Metro Line 7 to **"Higher Education Mega Center South Station"**, transfer to Line 4 to **"Dongchong Station"**.
        - **Option 3 (Departing from Guangzhou East Station/Guangzhou Station):** It is recommended to take the subway. Connect to Line 4 and get off at **"Dongchong Station"**.
- **Notes:**
    - **Subway Shuttle:** After arriving at **"Dongchong Station"**, you can take a bus (Nan 35, Nan 52, Nan 37, 37, etc.) or a taxi to the university, the distance is very short.
    - **Precise Destination:** When taking a taxi, be sure to confirm the destination is **"HKUST(GZ)"**.
    - **Avoid Peak Hours:** Guangzhou Metro and some sections of Nansha are very crowded and congested during morning (07:30-09:30) and evening (17:30-19:30) rush hours. Please allow sufficient time for a smooth journey.
- **More:** For more details, please refer to ["HKUST(GZ) Guidelines on Transportation to Arrive on Campus"](https://personal.hkust-gz.edu.cn/yeyutong/pdf/HKUSTGZ-Transportation.pdf).
